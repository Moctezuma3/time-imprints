    Our story

    Made in Ukraine. Felt everywhere.

    Time Imprints started in 2023 with a simple idea: the best gift isn't a thing — it's a moment you get to keep. Our kits turn an evening together into an artwork: your hands, your hugs, your kisses, printed in pearlescent color on canvas or cotton.

    • Skin-safe, hypoallergenic paint — safe for kids and pets
    • Everything in the box: canvas or tee, paints, brushes, instructions
    • Fast, tracked shipping worldwide

    From one black canvas to 58K of you.

    What started as one messy evening in Kyiv in 2023 became a small studio shipping moments worldwide. Here is how it happened.
