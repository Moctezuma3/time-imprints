    How do I order?

    Add a kit to your cart, choose your size, and check out — we confirm by email and ship the same week. You can note your paint colors at checkout.

    Is the paint safe on skin?

    Yes — hypoallergenic, made for skin contact, washes off with warm water. Safe around kids and pets.

    Can I wash the t-shirt afterwards?

    Yes. Once the fabric paint dries, it's fixed — machine wash like any tee.

    Do you ship to my country?

    Almost certainly: we ship worldwide from Ukraine with tracking. Shipping is free and calculated at checkout.

    Which paint colors can I pick?

    Canvas kits: orange, green, sky blue, gold, pink, silver — all pearlescent. T-shirt kits: black, white, red, sky blue, pink. Tell us your picks in the order notes.

    Any discounts?

    Every box includes a scratch card with a discount for your next kit — valid 6 months.
